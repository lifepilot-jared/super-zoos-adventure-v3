# Super Zoos Adventure V3.6 — Engine Build Contract

## Branch policy
- `main` remains the stable playable build.
- `develop` receives only verified milestones.
- `feature/v3.6-engine` contains the engine stabilisation work.
- No direct feature commits to `main`.

## Non-negotiable engine rules
1. One world clock owns all gameplay movement.
2. Obstacles, pickups, rails, tunnels, hills and route props consume the same world delta and speed.
3. Scenery may use parallax multipliers, but gameplay objects may not own separate timers.
4. The encounter director may expose only one collision decision gate at a time.
5. A damaging encounter may remove at most one heart.
6. Damage protection must remain active for at least 1.5 seconds after a hit.
7. Hazard rows require a safe escape lane.
8. Consecutive hazard gates require a minimum reaction gap.
9. Restart clears timers, effects, encounters and debug overrides.
10. Pause freezes world time, power-up time and phase transitions.

## Hero requirements
- Preserve the approved plush Peter and Judy presentation.
- Never replace approved characters with primitive placeholder models in a production build.
- Use local V3 assets before merge to `develop`.
- Every action must have a safe fallback pose.

## V3.6 engine acceptance test
- Five continuous minutes on iPad without a disappearing hero.
- No stationary gameplay object while other gameplay objects move.
- No overlapping double-heart loss.
- No unresolved item remaining at the player collision point.
- Pause and resume preserve the exact encounter state.
- Restart returns to a clean deterministic opening sequence.
- Debug panel can spawn each mechanic without waiting for natural progression.

## Merge gate
`feature/v3.6-engine` may merge into `develop` only after the acceptance test passes. `develop` may merge into `main` only after iPhone and iPad verification.
